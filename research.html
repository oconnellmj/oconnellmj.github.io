Current Research

Most of my research focuses on integrative dimension reduction methods for multi-source data. In many cases, the multiple sources are different types of omics data sets (e.g., RNA-seq, miRNA, and methylation data). However, the different sources can also be different subject sets of the same data type, such as data collected in different batches or different studies. I have also applied these methods in other data contexts, including sports and toxicology.
